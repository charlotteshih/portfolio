About Me

I am a graphic designer with a background in art history and a penchant for creative-yet-methodical problem solving that has evolved into a passion for programming. I am currently enrolled in Thinkful's Engineering Nights & Weekends bootcamp with the goal of becoming a full-stack developer. I love the collaborative nature of coding and am thrilled at the opportunity to learn new languages and collaborate on projects of all sorts.

When I'm not at my computer, you can find me... well... at my computer, playing Stardew Valley, guzzling tacos al pastor like my life depends on it on random street corners, checking out comedy shows around Los Angeles, or nerding out at the Natural History Museum or La Brea Tar Pits alongside children 1/3 my age.
